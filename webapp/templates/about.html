About PIARES

Historical Research Platform with Global Perspectives

Mission

PIARES (Platform for Integrated Academic Research and Educational Scholarship) is dedicated to providing comprehensive historical research with global perspectives. Our platform emphasizes cross-cultural analysis, primary source materials, and non-European viewpoints to create a more inclusive understanding of historical events and processes.

Research Topics

Our research covers 12 major historical topics:

  • Scientific Revolutions - Global knowledge transfer and innovation
  • Enlightenment - Cross-cultural philosophical developments
  • Early Modern Exploration - Global perspectives on exploration
  • Tokugawa Japan - Social and cultural transformations
  • Colonialism in Southeast Asia - Local responses and resistance
  • Ming & Qing Dynasties - Chinese imperial history
  • Haitian Revolution - Global impact and diaspora influences
  • Opposition to Imperialism - Resistance movements worldwide
  • World's Fairs & Zoos - Cultural display and colonial exhibition
  • Eugenics - Global scientific racism and resistance
  • Globalization & Dictatorships - Economic integration and authoritarianism
  • Decolonization - Independence movements and cultural revival

Methodology

Our research methodology emphasizes:

  • Primary source analysis from multiple cultural perspectives
  • Cross-cultural comparative approaches
  • Integration of non-Western scholarly traditions
  • Global historical connections and influences
  • Resistance to European-centric historical narratives

Data Sources

Research content is gathered from leading academic databases including:

  • Google Scholar
  • arXiv
  • JSTOR (planned)
  • Project MUSE (planned)
  • Regional academic databases
